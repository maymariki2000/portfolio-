Pilgrim Safety & Tracking Application

Innovation Project - Hajj Season

Context

During Hajj season, an estimated 200,000 pilgrims are reported lost each year, creating safety risks, operational pressure on campaign supervisors, and stress for pilgrims themselves due to crowd density and transportation complexity.

Objective

Design and deliver a smart tracking solution that ensures pilgrim safety, enables rapid assistance, and provides campaign supervisors with real-time visibility over their pilgrims' locations.

My Role

Front-End Developer using Flutter | UI/UX Designer using Figma | Team Lead coordinating development and delivery | Contributed to system integration with Firebase for real-time data and notifications.

Key Outputs

  • Real-Time Pilgrim-to-Bus Tracking
  • Supervisor Location Monitoring Dashboard
  • Assistance Request & Alert System
  • Departure Notification & Reminder System
